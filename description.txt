Allows enemies to spawn with prefixes, which can change their stats or add various special effects. 
See the mod homepage for a much more detailed description of content and a changelog.

New and interesting loot has been added - kill enemies with the "Rare" prefix to obtain it.

1.2.2 adds 6 variants of Needles, a new type of throwing weapon, craftable with tickets of each tier.
